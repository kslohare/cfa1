# 📚 Subject-wise Syllabus + Study Hours

## 1. Ethics & Professional Standards
**Weight:** 15–20%  
**Suggested Hours:** 45–55 hours

### Topics:
- Code of Ethics & Standards of Professional Conduct  
- Ethical decision-making framework  
- GIPS (Global Investment Performance Standards)

### Focus:
- Concept clarity + scenario-based questions  
- Revision multiple times (very important)

### 💡 Tip:
Ethics is like **rules of the game**—small mistakes can cost you big.

---

## 2. Quantitative Methods
**Weight:** 8–12%  
**Suggested Hours:** 30–40 hours

### Topics:
- Time Value of Money (TVM)
- Probability
- Hypothesis Testing
- Correlation & Regression

### Focus:
- Practice numericals daily
- Formula retention

### 💡 Tip:
Think of this as **math engine of finance**

---

## 3. Economics
**Weight:** 8–12%  
**Suggested Hours:** 30–35 hours

### Topics:
- Microeconomics (Demand & Supply)
- Macroeconomics (GDP, Inflation)
- Monetary & Fiscal Policy
- Currency Exchange Rates

### Focus:
- Concept understanding > memorization

## 4. Financial Statement Analysis (FSA)
**Weight:** 13–17%  
**Suggested Hours:** 55–65 hours (Highest)

- Financial Ratios
- Revenue Recognition
- Inventory & Long-lived assets
- Financial Reporting Standards

### Focus:
- Heavy practice + repeated revision

### 💡 Tip:
This is like **debugging a company’s financial health**
